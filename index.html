What it is

The private, all-in-one ledger.

The Yield Ledger is a private, offline-first finance app that helps you manage every part of your financial life — accounts, budgets, bills, investments, and credit — all in one secure desktop application.

It gives you a clear, real-time overview of your net worth, spending patterns, and financial health without relying on the cloud or sharing your data with anyone. Everything is encrypted, stored locally, and built to keep your money, your plan, and your privacy exactly where they belong: with you.
